What Do I Do?

I’m a programmer. After taking an introductory computer programming course back in my senior year of high school, I found coding to a be a powerful and fascinating skill. The satisfaction of building functional and working programs easily was worth the time it took to learn new programming techniques and to work the minor bugs that came up along the way. Coding has taught me to think things through thoroughly and carefully, but also to appreciate the ups and downs of the development process. If anything, I’ve learned that the things worth pursuing are things that aren’t easily accomplished on the first try.

At school, I’ve programmed extensively with Python and C++, and introduced to other tools like MySQL and Git. Currently, I’m trying my hand at web development (hence this website), and right now I’m trying to learn some web frameworks, starting with React.js. My experience continues to expand, through my college education and through self-learning. You can check some of things I work on in my Portfolio (under construction), or visit my Github .
