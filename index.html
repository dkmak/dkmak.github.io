SKILLS & INTERESTS

Experience

 Darryl builds front end customer experiences for Alias, the seller app for the GOAT marketplace. He supports over two million users who use the platform to sell premium clothing items easily and globally. He and his team make it easy for users to sell their items, and present them with visually engaging seller analytics data, like data-driven pricing and buying trends, that assist sellers in selling items optimally.

 Previously, Darryl worked as an Android developer on the Consumer Identity team at Capital One. He primarily worked on an Android SDK consumed by multiple Capital One mobile apps, designed to add an extra level of authenication to customers and allow millions of users to do banking safely and securely. He focused on improving authentication features such as One-Time Pin (OTP) and delivering the user interface for Presto, an authentication feature that utilized NFC technology on Capital One credit cards.
